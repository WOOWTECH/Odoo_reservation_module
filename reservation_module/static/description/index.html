Online Booking Flow

Step 1: Appointment List

Customers visit /appointment to browse all published appointment types.

Step 2: Appointment Detail

View appointment description, duration, and click "Book Now" to start.

Step 3: Date & Staff Selection

Choose a date from the calendar and optionally select a preferred staff member.

Step 4: Time Slot Selection

Available time slots based on staff availability, shown in configured intervals.

Step 5: Booking Form

Fill in name, email, phone, guests, and notes. Payment alert shown for paid types.

Step 6: Booking Confirmed

Confirmation page with booking reference, details, and email notification. Online meetings show "Join Meeting" button.

Join Meeting Button

Online meeting confirmations include a "Join Meeting" button linking to the Discuss channel.
