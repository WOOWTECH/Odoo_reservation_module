Features

Multiple Appointment Types

Meetings, video calls, table bookings, resource reservations, and paid consultations.

Resource & Staff Management

Manage availability of rooms, tables, equipment, and staff members.

Online Booking Portal

Beautiful public-facing booking pages for your customers.

Payment Integration

Collect payments upfront with Odoo's payment providers.

Auto Confirmation

Automatically confirm bookings based on capacity rules.

Email Notifications

Automatic confirmation and reminder emails.
